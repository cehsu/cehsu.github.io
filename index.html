About Me

I am a product-driven software engineer, dedicated to crafting delightful, intuitive user experiences, and building quickly and seamlessly with my team. I strive for a light touch both in terms of aesthetics and clear, minimalist code, and am always eager to experiment with new technologies and different design patterns.

I have experience building across the full stack for both web and mobile, including creating RESTful APIs, designing database schemas for both structured and semi-structured data, and engineering responsive, progressive and accessible user interfaces.

Some of my favorite tools for accomplishing this include: HTML5, CSS3, SCSS, JavaScript (ES5/6), Node.js/Express, jQuery, React, Angular, Git and Webpack.
